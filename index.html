About



  • How did we come up with the problem?

    After speaking with to several white-collar individuals, we began to realize the scarcity of their involvement in regard to donations to non-profit organizations, more specifically orphanages. This did not come about as a response to low funding or absence of willingness but rather the lack of practicality and time efficiency to do so. Our project is meant to create a user-friendly interface in which these individuals have access to orphanages’ Amazon wish lists and are then able to easily donate needed supply. It is an effortless manner for individuals to who do not have the time to personally deliver donations to lend a helpful hand to those in need and make a difference.

  • Who will benefit and how?

    We are not all qualified to personally make a difference in every child’s life, but we can all donate resources to those who are left with the task of doing so. This project will benefit orphanages which in turn directly benefit those who are most helpless around us. By giving a couple of minutes of our day these children will no longer suffer because of lack of resources consequently improving their quality of life.

  • Future development, expandability.

    Due to our project’s flexibility it can be further developed with little modification. Our hope is that in the future we will be able to broaden the scope of users to include immigration centers, and homeless and LGBTQ+ shelters. We believe any and all organizations that run solely on donations could immensely benefit from this tool.
